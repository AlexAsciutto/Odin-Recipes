Instant Pot Brown Rice

Description

Fill the instant pot with 3 cups of water per every cup of dry brown rice. Heat for 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • Brown Rice
  • Instant Pot
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse brown rice
  2. Put brown rice in instant pot
  3. Pour appropriate amount of water in pot
  4. Close lid and set for 20 minutes
  5. Let them sit for 30 minutes before releasing steam
