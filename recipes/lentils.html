Instant Pot Lentils

Description

Fill the instant pot with 2 cups of water per every cup of dry lentils. Heat for 6 minutes for firm lentils, 8 minutes for soft lentils, and 10 minutes for mushy lentils.

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Instant Pot
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse lentils
  2. Put lentils in instant pot
  3. Pour appropriate amount of water in pot
  4. Close lid and set desired time
  5. Let them sit for 10 minutes before releasing steam
